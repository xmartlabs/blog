The most important apple developers conference is coming. The 2013 edition will be starting next Monday, and apple will be introducing the new products and software features.


For this year, there is huge expectation about iOS 7, the most interesting change expected is a completely revamped UI and a lot of UX improvements that will offer many opportunities.

If you can't attend the event and want to be informed about everything that will be announced these 5 days, we strongly suggest to see the schedule here https://developer.apple.com/wwdc/schedule/ . You can also download the event application created by Apple from here:  https://itunes.apple.com/app/wwdc/id640199958 which will be frequently updated with videos of the talks and news.
